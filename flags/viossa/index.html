The Flag of Viossa (Viossa: Flakku fu Viossa) was created in 2016 by r/conlangs Klaus (u/clausangeloh on Reddit). It consists of a white circle superimposed on a blue background, with a bird in the center of the circle. The bird can be viewed as either a dove, or a pidgeon (a pun on the word pidgin, since Viossa is a conpidgin). The positioning of the bird is similar to the letter V, which is the first letter of Viossa. The blue used on the flag is hex #00bbff (rgb 0, 187, 255), and the white is #FFFFFF (rgb 255, 255, 255).
